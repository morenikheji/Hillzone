Direct Investments

Our Direct Investments are typically longer-dated, less liquid private market transactions and may be in various parts of the capital structure. We generally look for inefficiently financed situations where we can facilitate a recapitalization, or situations in which traditional institutional financial capital is not available. This strategy can include transitional financing in the form of senior secured and mezzanine loans, project and non-performing loan portfolios, convertible debt, event and growth equity.
